## Key Effects
Realistic shadows (layered), parallax (3–5 layers), smooth transitions 300–400ms, depth via backdrop-filter and layered box-shadow.

> **Project override:** No WebGL/Three.js. Achieve depth through: `box-shadow` with multiple layers, `backdrop-filter: blur()`, card lift on hover (`translateY + scale`), subtle gradient overlays on art.

## Avoid (Anti-patterns)
- Flat/minimalist design — cards should feel tactile and dimensional
- Static assets with no hover feedback
- Light backgrounds on card or admin surfaces
